Mailing Checklist

A checklist to help setup mailing jobs.

Size:

Size determines at what rate the piece will mail. The Longest side is considered the width. Ensure aspect ratio is more than 1.3 and less than 2.5. (Length/Height = Ratio)
Refer to the USPS template.

Letter (Including postcards, envelopes, cards, booklets and Self-Mailers)
Min. 3.5"x5" - Max 6.125"x11.5"
  • A letter size is always horizontal
  • Must be at least .009" thick
  • Maximum thickness is .25"
